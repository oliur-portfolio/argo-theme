Hyper-personalizationis upon us

Don’t mind us. We’re just peering over your shoulder as you read this, noting how many times you opened the email or clicked a link, the browser you’re using (), and your phone’s current OS ( - you should probably update that by the way.) Click 'Personalize Me' for more options.

These dark interactions exist for a reason: to enable hyper-personalized user experiences, interfaces customized uniquely for each one of us. Form Follows Me is defining our modern digital era of product design.

More and more companies are moving from the myopic view of personalization as an advertising device. They’re coming to us seeking deeper, more meaningful customization for customer interactions—for both the business and the user. As we look ahead to a new year, we’re reclaiming and redirecting personalization as an opportunity for meeting real customer needs.

Calm Follows Me

CalmFollows Me

IoT sensors and plug-and-play electronics have altered the manufacturing landscape, enabling interfaces that are mass-customized for individual users. Experiences that guide users across entire journeys, shaping conversations and connections and delivering bespoke interactions, will become the norm. Just beyond this sits sparser interfaces—mass customization means no more tabs, search, or filtering. The result is calmer, more streamlined, more ubiquitous UI.

Trust Follows Me

TrustFollows Me

We’re way beyond customized greetings. Hyper-personalization also means accuracy, presenting the exact files, apps, and sites you need for specific tasks at hand. Accuracy brings trust, an attribute we all agree needs more prevalence in digital existence. The right thinking in UI design can turn concerns into opportunities that we can use to adapt and improve our lives in a meaningful way.
